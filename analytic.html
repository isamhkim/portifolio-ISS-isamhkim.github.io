Final balance

During this 2 years of being a student at INSA Toulouse I've learned a lot and improved my competences not only in the engineering area but also in a personal aspect. I had the opportunity to work with people with different backgrounds and different cultures which made my training more interesting and complete.

Looking back at me at the begining of this internship and now getting to the end, from a technical aspect, I feel much more confident about my skills and competences. This specialisation in Innovative Smart Systems helped me realise my interest to continue in this domain and to deepen my learning in certain domains such as Big Data and Web Semantics. It also made me realise lots of weak points, in Security specially and some Network aspects, that I really want to get better at.

Finally, I'd like to give a special thanks to all the teachers who made it possible to have so much growth in so little time.
